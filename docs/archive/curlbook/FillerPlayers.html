What is a filler-player?


A filler-player is a player which is created automatically when the name is not know.  Stats and data for the player are only viewable through the ‘Team’ view.  The player will not show up in the ‘Players’ tab until its name has been changed.


How do I create one?


Simply create a team, leaving one or more of the positions un-filled:




Tapping ‘Save’ will bring up the following prompt:




Tapping ‘Continue’ will create a team with (in this case) 3 filler-players.


How do I view a filler-player?


A filler player does not show up in the ‘Players’ list.  To view them, you must navigate to their team detail page:




As with any team detail page, tapping the blue arrow beside a player name will bring up their detail view:





What do I do when I learn a filler-player’s name?


To change a filler-player into a normal player (with a normal name), navigate to their detail view (as shown above), and tap ‘Edit’:





From here, change the first and last names of the player, and tap ‘Save’:




The following prompt will be shown:




Tap ‘Change’ to change the player from filler to regular.  Their name will now show in the team, and the player will show in the ‘Players’ tab:
