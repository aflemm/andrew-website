To view stats by hammer, first navigate to a ‘Stats options view’:




To do this, see ‘Viewing game stats’, or ‘Viewing team stats’.  This stat option is not available from a player’s stats.


To view hammer stats, tap ‘Hammer’.




The control at the top of the screen toggles between with/without the hammer:




Based upon the selection in the with/without control, you are shown the team’s overall throwing percentage with/without the hammer, as well as the team’s scoring efficiency.


To view more detailed stats in ends with/without the hammer (again, based upon the selection at the top of the screen), tap ‘Shot type’ or ‘Miss type’.  For information on these stat views, see ‘Stats by shot-type’, and ‘Stats by miss-type’.
