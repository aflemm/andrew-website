First, from the ‘Clubs’ tab, tap the ‘+’ button in the top right corner:




Next, the club name must be entered:




The second field is the number of sheets:




Finally, the colours of the rocks at the club need to be set.  Scroll down, and tap ‘Colours’:

 



Here, you must select two colours, and tap ‘Save’.




Once the colours are selected, the club can be saved.  Tap ‘Save’.  After saving a club, all of its data may be edited at any time.




Once the club is saved, it will appear in the ‘Clubs’ tab:
