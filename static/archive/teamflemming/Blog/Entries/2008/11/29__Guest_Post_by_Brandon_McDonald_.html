Diaries of a teenaged second

 

*Guest Post by Brandon McDonald*

Saturday, November 29, 2008

 

I hate most things.


First off, it is my job to set the table - our coach told me so - metaphorically speaking of course. Without those first two rocks, we wouldn’t be where we are now. I am kind of a big deal. Much like now, and with me setting the table, I will commence with this post.


I don’t really like these guys. They are clowns. Mike, ha, he wears shirts that are too small. Ed; he’s like a sheep dog. He needs to cut his hair. (and I’m not saying that because I cut my hair short, fyi I’m not balding, it’s just short) And Andrew, what could I say that hasn’t already been said. He thinks that we’re friends, I am reluctant to tell him otherwise.


I try to be a sheltered person in the curling world, this is completely opposite to my university life. I go to clubs, I dance, I’m a fan of Kanye and the like. Futhermore I rock my Band of Brothers jeans, wicked Air Force Ones and clutch rock&republic shirts. Unknown to the guys I roll in a souped up Jag. I like to spin up and down Bankside, O’Connor and Wellington in Ohh’town....and I don’t call it that cause the ladies love my moves......but it adds to the ambiance.


Cutlery. Check. Napkins. Yup. Salt n Pepper. You know it!

Now that we have the preliminaries out of the way. The rest of the team may proceed.


Peace out.

B Wheels McD.
